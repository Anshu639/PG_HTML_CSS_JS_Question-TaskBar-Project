Introduction
Welcome to the Pig Game, a fun and simple dice game where two players compete to reach 30 points first. This document will guide you through setting up and playing the game.

Setup Instructions
1. Download the Game Files: Ensure you have all the necessary files, including `index.html`, `style.css`, `script.js`, `script1.js`, and the `images` folder containing dice images (`dice-1.png` to `dice-6.png`).

2. Open the Game: Load `index.html` in a modern web browser to start the game.

Game Interface
- Player Sections: Two sections, each representing a player, display the current and total scores.
- Dice Image: Shows the result of the latest dice roll.
- Buttons:
  - Roll Dice: Rolls the dice.
  - Hold: Saves the current score to the total and switches the turn.
  - New Game: Resets the game to the initial state.

How to Play
1. Starting the Game: Click the "New Game" button. Player 1 starts the game.

2. Rolling the Dice: Players take turns. On your turn, click "Roll Dice" to roll the dice.

3. Scoring:
   - If the dice shows a number between 2 and 6, that number is added to your current score.
   - If the dice shows 1, your turn ends, and you lose your current score for that turn.

4. Holding: Click "Hold" to add your current score to your total score and end your turn.

5. Winning the Game: The first player to reach or exceed 30 points in total wins the game. The game ends automatically, and the winner's section is highlighted.

6. Restarting the Game: To play again, click the "New Game" button.

Additional Information
- Game Rules: The Pig Game is a game of risk and reward. Decide whether to risk rolling again for a higher score or hold to secure your points.
- Browser Compatibility: The game is best experienced in modern web browsers like Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.

Enjoy playing the Pig Game!
